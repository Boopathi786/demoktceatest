KCEA

We are KCEA was formed by group of Engineers on 12th march 2018 for a reason of resource utilization. we have joined with FACEAT in 4th region as 11th association. We have founded our own rules and regulation and registered as KCEA on 13th March 2019 through an auditor Mr. Mahendran. Our registration No is Karur / SR - 18 / 2019. We have our association Bank Account in Tamilnadu Mercantile Bank. We have our executive Committe Meeting Every Month.

To Promote friendship and establish unity in Profession. To encourge welfare of our society common social responsibility and conducting rallies, seminars, special programme for awareness of social responsibility.
